Meet Our Faculty

Our college is home to a dedicated team of educators who are committed to providing quality education. Below is a list of the key faculty positions:

  • Principal: The head of the college, responsible for administration and academic activities.
  • Vice-Principal: Assists the principal and helps manage policies, discipline, and overall functioning.
  • Head of Departments (HODs): Leads academic departments and is responsible for the department's activities and curriculum.
  • Professors: Senior faculty members who have extensive knowledge in their field and lead advanced teaching and research.
  • Associate Professors: Experienced faculty who contribute to both teaching and research, typically with a few years of experience less than professors.
  • Assistant Professors: Junior faculty members responsible for undergraduate teaching and assisting with research work.
  • Lecturers: Faculty who handle undergraduate lectures for students in various disciplines.
  • Lab Assistants: Provide support to students during practical sessions in labs and maintain the equipment.
  • Librarian: Manages the college library and ensures access to study resources and reference materials.
  • Administrative Staff: Handle college administration tasks, such as student records, admissions, and coordination.
  • Sports Coach: Trains students in various sports and manages extracurricular activities related to fitness and competition.
  • Counselor: Offers career advice and psychological support to students to help them manage stress and academic concerns.
